Once there was a small village near a silent forest.
In that village lived a curious boy named Arjun.
Arjun loved asking questions about everything.
He believed the world was full of hidden stories.
Every morning he woke up before sunrise.
He liked watching the sky change colors.
One day he noticed an old path in the forest.
No one in the village talked about that path.
People said it led to nothing.
But Arjun believed every path leads somewhere.
His curiosity grew stronger every day.
Finally he decided to explore the path.
He packed a small bag with food and water.
His mother warned him to be careful.
Arjun promised he would return before sunset.
With excitement in his heart he entered the forest.
The trees were tall and mysterious.
Birds were singing beautiful songs.
The wind whispered through the leaves.
Arjun felt both scared and excited.
After walking for a while he found an old signboard.
The signboard was broken and faded.
It pointed toward a deeper part of the forest.
Arjun continued walking with courage.
Soon he reached a small river.
The water was clear and peaceful.
He sat there and rested for a moment.
Suddenly he saw something shining in the water.
He stepped closer to see it clearly.
It was a small golden key.
Arjun wondered what the key could open.
He kept the key safely in his pocket.
Then he continued his journey.
The forest started becoming darker.
The sounds around him slowly changed.
He heard strange noises from far away.
But he kept walking bravely.
Soon he found an old wooden door.
The door stood alone between two huge trees.
There was no wall around it.
It looked like a door to nowhere.
Arjun remembered the golden key.
He slowly took the key out.
His hands trembled with curiosity.
He inserted the key into the lock.
The lock opened with a soft click.
Arjun slowly pushed the door open.
Behind the door was a beautiful hidden garden.
The garden was full of colorful flowers.
Butterflies were flying everywhere.
A peaceful silence filled the air.
In the center stood a wise old man.
The old man smiled kindly at Arjun.
He said he had been waiting for someone curious.
Arjun was surprised and confused.
The old man explained the secret.
He said curiosity opens magical doors.
Most people stop asking questions.
But curious people discover hidden worlds.
Arjun listened carefully to every word.
The old man gave him a small book.
The book was filled with blank pages.
He said Arjun must fill it with discoveries.
Every adventure should be written in it.
Arjun felt proud and excited.
He promised to keep exploring the world.
The sun slowly started to set.
Arjun thanked the old man respectfully.
He walked back through the magical door.
When he turned around the door disappeared.
Only the forest remained.
Arjun began walking toward the village.
The forest no longer felt scary.
It felt like a place full of secrets.
Soon he reached the small river again.
He washed his face with the cool water.
Then he continued walking home.
The sky was turning orange and purple.
Birds were flying back to their nests.
Arjun reached the edge of the village.
His mother was waiting worriedly.
She was relieved to see him safe.
Arjun hugged her tightly.
He told her about his strange adventure.
She smiled and listened carefully.
That night Arjun opened the blank book.
He wrote about the golden key.
He wrote about the magical garden.
He wrote about the wise old man.
His first adventure filled many pages.
But he knew it was only the beginning.
Many paths were still unexplored.
Many questions were still unanswered.
Arjun promised himself one thing.
He would never stop being curious.
Because curiosity creates amazing stories.
And every story begins with a single step.
